About Our Organization

Discover the heart of our student-led governance and our commitment to the Vincentian community.

SVCI Senior High School Supreme Student Government

The Supreme Student Government of the Senior High School Department of St. Vincent's College Inc., imploring the aid of Almighty God, we are called to serve a community of Vincentians, fully aware of our duties and responsibilities to God, to San Vicente Ferrer, to the school, as well as to our Oath of Office. We hereby promulgate this Constitution and By-Laws as a declaration of our full service to bring camaraderie among all members of the campus, to protect and defend our rights as student leaders, to preserve the image of the school and our fellow Vincentians, and to uphold our duties by deterring future inconsistencies.

Our Mission, Vision & Core Values

The guiding principles that drive our organization to serve the Vincentian community with excellence and integrity.

Our Vision

Inspiring our future, shaping our legacy

St. Vincent's College Incorporated, a Diocesan Catholic School, envisions a FULLY INTEGRATED PERSON guided by love, truth and freedom in the service of God, Country and People.

Our Mission

Guiding our actions, defining our purpose

St. Vincent's College Incorporated Basic Education Department through its Catholic Christian ideals commits itself to the tasks of:

  • 1

    Pursuing excellent and globally competitive Basic Education for young men and women who will contribute to the total development of the family, human society and the church;

  • 2

    Integrating the motto "Veritas Liberabit Vos" and the values of Saint Vincent Ferrer in teaching and service to the community especially to the least privileged; and

  • 3

    Molding the character of Basic Education students committed to the life and teachings of Jesus Christ.
