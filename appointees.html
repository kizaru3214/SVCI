About the Appointees Division

Our Role and Responsibilities

  • Assist in implementing various SSG programs and projects
  • Support committee heads in their respective functions
  • Execute assigned tasks from different SSG committees
  • Serve as liaisons between students and committee heads
  • Provide innovative ideas for student welfare and development
