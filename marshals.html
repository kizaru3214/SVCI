About the Cadet Officers Division

Our Platoon Specializations

Marshal’s Duty

The Marshals provide manpower support to ensure safety, order, and the smooth flow of school events while upholding discipline and peace within the campus.

Roles and Responsibilities

• Maintain peace and order during school events

• Enforce school rules and the Code of Conduct

• Assist in crowd control during assemblies and programs

• Serve as ushers in official functions

• Promote discipline and respect in the Vincentian community

Core Values

• Convictium – Discipline with dignity and respect

• Servicio – Service with honor and integrity

• Gloria – Excellence, accountability, and pride in service

Motto

"Discipline with Dignity, Service with Honor"

Marshal’s Pledge

We commit to discipline, integrity, and service to the Vincentian community.
